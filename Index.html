Vision & Mission

Our Vision

"To be a premier international innovative university committed to excellence in teaching, research, and knowledge transfer."

Our Mission

  • Quality education in engineering, technology, management, pharmacy.
  • Promote research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
  • Industry-academia collaboration for employability.
  • Holistic student development.
  • Inclusive education for all sections of society.
  • Sustainable technologies for societal challenges.

GTU Act / Regulations

The Gujarat Technological University Act, 2007

Established under Gujarat Act No. 20 of 2007. Assent of Governor on 16th May 2007.

Notification: GH/SH/4/GTU/2007/1208/07 dated 16th May 2007.

Key Provisions

  • Section 3: Establishment with jurisdiction over Gujarat.
  • Section 4: Powers — affiliating, examining, degrees.
  • Section 12: Board of Governors.
  • Section 16: Academic Council.
  • Section 20: Finance Committee.
  • Section 28: Power to make statutes.

Vice - Chancellor
VC Photo

Dr. Rajul K. Gajjar

Vice-Chancellor, GTU Ahmedabad (appointed 3 August 2023 — first woman to hold the position)

Dr. Gajjar holds a Ph.D. in Structural Engineering from M.S. University, Baroda, with over four decades of experience in academia, research, and administration. She previously served as Principal of L.D. College of Engineering and as Additional Commissioner, Education Department, Government of Gujarat.

"Committed to transforming GTU into a globally recognized centre of academic and technological excellence."
